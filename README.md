# Credit Card Financial Analysis - Tableau Dashboards

## 📌 Project Overview

This project leverages Tableau to provide a 360-degree view of credit card operations. It is divided into two primary reports to help stakeholders understand both the **demographic profile** of the customer base and the **financial performance** of transaction activities.

## 🗂️ Reports Included

### 1. Credit Card Customer Report (`.twbx`)

This dashboard focuses on the "Who" and "Why" behind the customer base. It is designed to identify high-value segments and monitor customer retention.

* 
**Customer Demographics**: Analysis by Age Group, Gender, Education Level, and Marital Status.


* 
**Financial Health**: Insights into Credit Limits and Utilization Ratios.


* 
**Churn Analysis**: Tracking "Attrited" vs. "Existing" customers to identify segments at risk of leaving the service.


* 
**Card Tiers**: Distribution of customers across Blue, Silver, Gold, and Platinum categories.



### 2. Credit Card Transaction Report (`.twbx`)

This dashboard focuses on the "How much" and "Where," providing deep insights into revenue generation and spending behavior.

* 
**Revenue Performance**: Tracking Total Revenue, Transaction Counts, and Interest Earned.


* 
**Spend Categories**: Breakdown of expenditures across Bills, Entertainment, Fuel, Grocery, Food, and Travel.


* 
**Transaction Methods**: Analysis of how customers pay, including Swipe, Chip, and Online transactions.


* 
**Temporal Trends**: Revenue analysis segmented by Week, Month, and Quarter.



## 🚀 Key Business Insights

* 
**High-Revenue Segments**: Identify which card tiers (e.g., Blue vs. Platinum) and demographic groups contribute the most to the bottom line.


* 
**Spending Patterns**: Discover which categories (like Groceries or Bills) have the highest transaction volumes.


* 
**Attrition Triggers**: Understand if certain demographic factors correlate with higher customer churn.



## 🛠️ Technical Implementation

* **Tool**: Tableau Desktop / Tableau Public.
* **File Format**: `.twbx` (Packaged Workbook containing data extracts).
* **Data Sources**: Integrated financial datasets and customer CRM records.

## 📖 How to Interact with the Dashboards

1. **Download** the `.twbx` files from this repository.
2. **Open** them using [Tableau Desktop](https://www.tableau.com/products/desktop) or [Tableau Reader](https://www.tableau.com/products/reader).
3. **Filters**: Use the interactive sidebars (Quarter, Gender, Card Category, etc.) to drill down into specific data segments.
